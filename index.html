About Me

Hello! I'm Dr. Bidyapati Thangjam, a Research Associate at ICAR, Umiam, Meghalaya, and a passionate E-Agriculture expert dedicated to bridging the gap between traditional farming and modern technology. With years of experience in digital agriculture solutions, I help farmers and agricultural businesses leverage technology for sustainable growth.

My mission is to empower farmers with knowledge about government schemes, digital tools, and modern farming techniques that increase productivity while preserving our environment for future generations.

I specialize in implementing government agricultural projects, providing training on digital farming tools, and connecting farmers with essential resources and benefits.
